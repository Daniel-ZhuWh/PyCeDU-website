METALS Program

The Masters in Education Technology and Applied Learning Science (METALS) is an 1-year professional Masters degree at Carnegie Mellon University. The program is situated between design, computer science, and psychology drawing on areas of all three to inform better design, creation and evaluation of learning technologies.
